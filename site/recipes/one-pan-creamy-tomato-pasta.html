One-Pan Creamy Tomato Pasta

Weeknight-friendly pasta in one pan.

Servings: 4 servings · Total time: PT25M
30-min one-pan

Ingredients

  • 300 g short pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 250 ml water or stock
  • 100 ml cream (or coconut milk)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Handful basil, torn
  • 30 g grated parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in oil 2–3 min.
  2. Add tomatoes, water/stock, salt and pepper; bring to simmer.
  3. Add pasta and cook uncovered 10–12 min, stirring.
  4. Stir in cream and basil; simmer 2 min.
  5. Serve with parmesan.
