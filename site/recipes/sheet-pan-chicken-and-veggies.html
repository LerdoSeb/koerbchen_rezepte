Sheet-Pan Chicken & Veggies

Hands-off tray bake for busy nights.

Servings: 4 servings · Total time: PT40M
tray-bake kid-friendly

Ingredients

  • 600 g chicken thighs, cubed
  • 500 g potatoes, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, wedges
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges to serve

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Toss all ingredients with oil and spices on a sheet pan.
  3. Roast 25–30 min until chicken is cooked and potatoes are tender.
  4. Serve with lemon.
